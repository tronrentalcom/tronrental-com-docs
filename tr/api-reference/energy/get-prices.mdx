## Fiyatlari Al

Enerji ve bant genisligi icin guncel piyasa fiyatlarini dondurur.

**Bu uc nokta herkese aciktir — kimlik dogrulama gerektirmez.**

### Yanit

<ResponseField name="energy_trx" type="object">
  65.000 birim icin TRX cinsinden enerji fiyati, sure anahtarina gore (`1h`)
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="energy_sun" type="object">
  1 birim basina SUN cinsinden enerji fiyati, sure anahtarina gore (`1h`)
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="energy_usd" type="object">
  65.000 birim icin USD cinsinden enerji fiyati, sure anahtarina gore (`1h`)
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="energy_volume" type="number">
  Enerji fiyati hesaplamasi icin kullanilan hacim (65.000)
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="bandwidth_trx" type="object">
  350 birim icin TRX cinsinden bant genisligi fiyati, sure anahtarina gore (`1h`)
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="bandwidth_sun" type="object">
  1 birim basina SUN cinsinden bant genisligi fiyati, sure anahtarina gore (`1h`)
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="bandwidth_usd" type="object">
  350 birim icin USD cinsinden bant genisligi fiyati, sure anahtarina gore (`1h`)
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="bandwidth_volume" type="number">
  Bant genisligi fiyati hesaplamasi icin kullanilan hacim (350)
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="trx_usd_rate" type="string">
  Guncel TRX/USD doviz kuru
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="burn_cost_trx" type="string">
  65.000 enerji icin TRX yakma maliyeti (kiralama olmadan)
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="burn_cost_usd" type="string">
  Ayni yakma maliyeti USD cinsinden
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="savings_percent" type="string">
  Kiralama ile yakma arasindaki tasarruf yuzdesi
</ResponseField>
