## Get Prices

Returns current market prices for energy and bandwidth.

**This endpoint is public — no authentication required.**

### Response

<ResponseField name="energy_price_sun" type="number">
  Current energy price in SUN per unit
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="energy_price_1k_trx" type="string">
  Price for 1,000 energy units in TRX
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="bw_price_1k_trx" type="string">
  Price for 1,000 bandwidth units in TRX
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="trx_usd_rate" type="string">
  Current TRX/USD exchange rate
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="burn_sun_per_energy" type="number">
  Network burn rate — SUN per energy unit (currently 100)
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="min_energy" type="number">
  Minimum energy amount per order
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="max_energy" type="number">
  Maximum energy amount per order
</ResponseField>
